About the Producer

I_AM_LEGEND has been producing music since he was in school. In high school he would make beats inside of his film and media classes and email them so that he could listen to them at home. He has worked with a few artists and has competed in beat competitions. What makes this producer stand out is his ability to take a sound, warp it, and turn it into something enjoyable to hear. He's got a bright future ahead of him. Some say he's the next Kanye West.
